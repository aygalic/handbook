    Model Selection¶

    Criteria¶

    1. AIC: -2l() + 2p
    2. BIC: -2l() + p log(n)
    3. Deviance: Measure of model fit

    Stepwise Procedures¶

    1. Forward Selection: Add terms sequentially
    2. Backward Elimination: Remove terms sequentially
    3. Stepwise: Combination of forward and backward
